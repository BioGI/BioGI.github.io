Strain rate tensor

Defined as half the sum of the velocity gradient tensor and its transpose:

E = \(\begin{bmatrix} \frac{\partial u}{\partial x} & \frac{1}{2}(\frac{\partial u}{\partial y} + \frac{\partial v}{\partial x}) & \frac{1}{2}(\frac{\partial u}{\partial z} + \frac{\partial w}{\partial x}) \\ \frac{1}{2}(\frac{\partial v}{\partial x} + \frac{\partial u}{\partial y}) & \frac{\partial v}{\partial y} & \frac{1}{2}(\frac{\partial v}{\partial z} + \frac{\partial w}{\partial y}) \\ \frac{1}{2}(\frac{\partial w}{\partial x} + \frac{\partial u}{\partial z}) & \frac{1}{2}(\frac{\partial w}{\partial y} + \frac{\partial v}{\partial z}) & \frac{\partial w}{\partial z} \end{bmatrix}\)

Second Invariant of the Symmetrical Strain Rate Tensor; E:

\[ \begin{equation*} II_E =\frac{1}{2} (E_{ii} E_{jj} - E_{ij} E_{ij}) \end{equation*} \] \[ \begin{equation*} II_E =\frac{1}{2} \Big( \big[E_{11} E_{22} + E_{11} E_{33} + E_{22} E_{33} \big] - \big[ E_{12} E_{12} + E_{13} E_{13} + E_{23} E_{23} \big] \Big) \end{equation*} \] Computing S to be used in Sherwood number correlations: \[ \begin{equation*} S = \sqrt{\frac{II_E}{2}} \end{equation*} \] Summerizing: \[ \begin{equation*} S =\frac{1}{2} \sqrt{\big[E_{11} E_{22} + E_{11} E_{33} + E_{22} E_{33}\big] - \big[E_{12} E_{12} + E_{13} E_{13} + E_{23} E_{23} \big]} \end{equation*} \]
