Solubility for in-vivo simulations:

For in-vivo simulations, assuming \(ph \approx 5.5\), we get: \[ \begin{equation*} \begin{split} \frac{S_T}{S_o} = \bigg( 1+ \frac{10^{-pKa}}{10^{-pH}} \bigg)= \bigg( 1+ \frac{10^{-4.4}}{10^{-5.5}} \bigg)= 13.589 \\ S_T = 13.589 \times 68 \frac{\mu g}{cm^3} = 924.1 \frac{\mu g}{cm^3} \\ S_T= 4.485 \frac{\mu m}{cm^3} \end{split} \end{equation*} \]
